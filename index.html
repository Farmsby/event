The agriculture sector has long been neglected in Nigeria. This event has been created to provide an inclusive community for the agro-ecosystem by presenting opportunities in the agricultural sectors to both farmers and investors, and to address issues faced by farmers in the 21st century, ultimately, improving the agricultural value chain in Nigeria.
